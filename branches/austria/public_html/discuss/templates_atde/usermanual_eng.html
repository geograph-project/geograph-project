New topics and replies

The new topic form is at the bottom of each forum listing page. Simply type a title for your topic and the message you wish to post.

To reply to an existing topic, use the form at the end of the topic listing

Automatic links highlighting

If you are using links like http://www.minibb.net (beginning with "http://", "https://", " ftp://", "www" or "mailto:"), they are automatically converted to "real" links. To prevent this, check the "disable formatting" box.

BB Codes

You cannot include HTML in posts or topics. You can add basic formatting with 'BB codes' in a similar fashion to many other forums.

The following codes are supported

  • [url=http://www.mylink.com] link text [/url] will make the link text hyperlinked to the given url.
  • [email=person@whatever.com] link text [/email] will make the link text hyperlinked to the given email address as a mailto: link
  • [img] http://www.foo.com/images/image.gif [/img] where http://www.foo.com/images/image.gif is the URL to an image file. You can also use [imgleft] or [imgright] to align your image with the left or right margin of your post.
  • [b] bold text [/b] highlights the enclosed text in bold
  • [i] italic text [/i] highlights the enclosed text in italics.
  • [u] underlined [/u] underlines the enclosed text

Note that when editing posts there are some handy buttons to help with inserting these BB codes. Also, if you wish to disable BB codes, perhaps to preserve some text which looks like a BB code, check the "disable formatting" box before posting.

Quoting messages

You can quickly quote from an existing message by highlighting the text you wish to copy and clicking the "quote" link

Although there is no [quote] BB code, you can use italics [i] to highlight quoted text.

Locking topics

Topic authors can lock their own topics, if they wish. At the bottom of the topic's page he must see small "lock topic" link. If clicked, the topic is closed and no further posts can be made to it.

Nobody except topic author or administrator can lock the topic. If the topic was locked by admin, topic author will be unable to unlock it

Editing messages

Users can edit their messages after posting, but after a period of time messages are frozen and cannot be edited. You cannot delete messages, but you can remove all the text if necessary.

Forums search panel

You can quickly search forums for keywords. While in the discussion area of the site, the search box will search within topics and posts, and also let you refine your search with more advanced options

Search results are sorted by post time in descending order (new posts are shown first).

Note that the search term must be at least 3 characters long, and less then 100

From the search results you can refine your search with further options. You can choose "match whole words" option which means you will search only WHOLE words (if you type "nick", the sentences containing "nick" will be found, but not words like "nickname" or "snickers"). Also, you can search within defined period of days when posts were made. If you want to expand your search, it is always better to set this to large amount, e.g. of 365 days. Note that searching for username is NOT affected by this "search-within-days" option.

Search within specified forums is also available. You can either search in all forums or choose a forum you want to search in.
