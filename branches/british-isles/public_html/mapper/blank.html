Don't forget you can drag and pan the map, and zoom in/out.

  • Click on the map once to freeze the Grid Reference display, and load thumbnails
  • Click anywhere on the map to change location
  • Click the same location again or the X beside the grid reference to unfreeze the Grid Reference Display.

You can choose whether clicking the map will show photos in that gridsquare or centisquare; the selector is under the map.

To switch layers, click the in top right of map.

Gridsquare Coverage
- Shows coverage by 1km squares, shows number of photos.

TPoint Availability
- Shown in purple are squares without a recent photo.

Centisquare Coverage
- Coverage by 100m squares. Colour key at bottom of page.

ScenicOrNot Data
- colour coded representation of scenicness. 1-10 scale, lighter/lower = more scenic.

Do not use the browser back button.
